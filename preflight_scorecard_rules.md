# MTSU Preflight Scorecard Rules

Source: Preflight Worksheet scorecard PDF provided by user.

## Weather

Use the most significant current and forecast weather for departure, enroute, and arrival.

### Total Wind Speed - Dual

| Condition | Score |
|---|---:|
| 0-9.9 kt | 0 |
| 10-19 kt | 2 |
| 20+ kt | 5 |

### Total Crosswind Component - Dual

| Condition | Score |
|---|---:|
| < 5 kt | 0 |
| 5-9 kt | 2 |
| 10-20 kt DA20/DA40 | 5 |

### Visibility

| Condition | Score |
|---|---:|
| 5+ SM | 0 |
| 3-4.9 SM | 3 |
| 1-2.9 SM | 5 |
| < 1 SM | 8 |

### Ceilings

| Condition | Score |
|---|---:|
| 5,000 ft+ | 0 |
| 3,000-4,999 ft | 2 |
| 1,500-2,999 ft | 3 |
| 500-1,499 ft | 5 |
| < 500 ft | 8 |

## Fuel And Flight Time

### Fuel Remaining At Landing

| Condition | Score |
|---|---:|
| >= 1.5 hr at cruise | 0 |
| 1.0-1.49 hr at cruise | 3 |
| < 1.0 hr at cruise | Approval Required +5 |

### Flight Time

| Condition | Score |
|---|---:|
| 0-2.9 hr | 0 |
| 3.0-4.9 hr | 2 |
| 5.0+ hr | 5 |

## Instructor Time

### 24-Hour Flight Instruction By End Of Flight

| Condition | Score |
|---|---:|
| < 5 hr | 0 |
| 5-5.9 hr | 2 |
| 6-6.9 hr | 4 |
| 7+ hr | Approval Required +6 |

### PIC/Instructor Duty Day

Use the most restrictive between student and instructor. Duty day begins with first scheduled activity and ends with a rest period consisting of 8 consecutive hours away from scheduled activities.

| Condition | Score |
|---|---:|
| 10-12 hr at scheduled return time | 3 |
| > 12 hr at scheduled return time | 5 |

## Cirrus - Icing, Altitude, And Oxygen Use

These rules appear on the scorecard but are not directly applicable to the current DA-40 app unless the preflight assistant later supports Cirrus aircraft.

| Condition | Score |
|---|---:|
| Climb/descent through known or forecast icing | 5 |
| Cruise in known or forecast icing | Approval Required +10 |
| Flight into forecast SLD | Prohibited |
| Cruise 12,500-18,000 MSL | 3 |
| Cruise > 18,000 MSL | Approval Required +8 |
| Duration on oxygen < 1 hr | 1 |
| Duration on oxygen 1-3 hr | 3 |
| Duration on oxygen > 3 hr | 5 |
